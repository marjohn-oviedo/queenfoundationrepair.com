About Queen Foundation Repair

We fix what's under your home — and we do it right.

What We Do

We specialize in the work that keeps your home standing strong. Foundation repair, basement waterproofing, crawl space repair and encapsulation, sump pump installation, and French drain systems. If it's below your home, we handle it.

Every job starts with a free estimate — no pressure, no sales pitch. We look at the problem, tell you what needs to happen, and give you a straightforward price. Then it's your call.

We don't cut corners. We don't pad invoices. And we don't disappear after the work is done. That's why every repair comes with a warranty. If something isn't right, we come back and fix it.

Who We Are

We started Queen Foundation Repair for one reason: homeowners deserve better. Better service, better communication, and better results when their home needs work.

We've seen too many companies show up late, give confusing quotes, and leave homeowners guessing. That's not how we operate. When you call us, we pick up. When we schedule an estimate, we show up. When we give you a price, that's the price.

Whether it's a cracked foundation, a wet basement, or a crawl space that needs attention — we've seen it, we've fixed it, and we stand behind every job with a warranty. This isn't a side hustle. This is what we do every day.
