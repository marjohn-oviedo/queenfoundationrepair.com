## SERVICE CARD DESCRIPTIONS

**Foundation Repair:**
Cracks in your walls? Doors not closing right? We find the cause and fix it so your home is stable again.

**Basement Waterproofing:**
Water in your basement ruins everything it touches. We stop it from getting in and keep it out for good.

**Crawl Space Repair:**
Sagging floors, musty smells, and moisture underneath your home. We fix the structure and seal it up.

**Crawl Space Encapsulation:**
A full vapor barrier system that locks out moisture, mold, and pests. Your crawl space stays clean and dry.

**Sump Pump Installation:**
A sump pump catches water before it floods your basement. We install reliable systems that run when you need them.

**French Drain Installation:**
Water pooling around your foundation causes damage over time. A French drain moves it away from your home before it becomes a problem.

## FAQ

**Subtext:**
Got questions? Here are the ones we hear most from homeowners.

**Q1:** What areas do you serve?
**A1:** We serve homeowners across multiple states, with crews in North Carolina, South Carolina, Ohio, Georgia, Alabama, Tennessee, and Pennsylvania. If you're not sure whether we cover your area, give us a call and we'll let you know.

**Q2:** Do you offer free estimates?
**A2:** Yes. Every estimate is free, and there's no obligation. We'll come out, look at the problem, explain what's going on, and give you a straightforward price. No pressure, no sales pitch.

**Q3:** How do I know if my home needs foundation repair?
**A3:** The most common signs are cracks in your walls or floors, doors and windows that stick or won't close, and uneven or sloping floors. If you've noticed any of these, it's worth getting it looked at before the damage gets worse.

**Q4:** How long does the work usually take?
**A4:** It depends on the job. Most foundation repairs take one to three days. Basement waterproofing and crawl space work can range from a day to a few days depending on the size of the space and what needs to be done. We'll give you a timeline before we start.

**Q5:** Do you offer financing or payment plans?
**A5:** We work with homeowners to make the process as affordable as possible. Give us a call to talk through your options. We'd rather help you get the work done now than wait until the problem gets bigger and more expensive.
